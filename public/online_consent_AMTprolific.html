Project Title: Cognitive and Molecular Challenges to Statistical Inference Across Healthy Aging.
Project Sponsors: Brown University and the National Institute on Aging
Principal-Investigator: Matthew R Nassar, Ph.D.

PURPOSE: The study examines how learning, memory and decision making change with age and to better understand the reason.

PROCEDURES: You will be asked to complete several computerized tasks. You may be asked to complete brief surveys, medical history questions, and demographic questions on the computer.

TIME INVOLVED: The study will involve X sessions, each lasting approximately X minutes of your time.

BENEFITS: There are no direct benefits to you from participating in this research study.
